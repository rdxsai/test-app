Creating Effective Learning Objectives

Well-written objectives help align content, assessment, and student expectations.

  • Use clear, measurable verbs and specific outcomes.
  • Keep each objective focused on one skill or competency.
  • Write objectives that can be directly assessed by quiz questions.
Tip: Start with an action verb and describe what success looks like.
