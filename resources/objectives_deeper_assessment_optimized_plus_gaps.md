# Consolidated Accessibility Objectives (Assessment-Optimized + Gaps)


## I.A

### I.A.1
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Compare the scope, purpose, and relationship of WCAG 2.0, WCAG 2.1, WCAG 2.2, ATAG 2.0, and EN 301 549, and identify how each influences web accessibility design and conformance.

### I.A.10
**Cognitive Level:** Explain
Explain how ATAG 2.0 applies to web content authoring tools and describe the intent of its two main parts.

### I.A.11
**Cognitive Level:** Evaluate
Distinguish between accessibility features that authoring tools can automate and those requiring author input, and evaluate the benefits and limitations of automated authoring support.

### I.A.12
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Identify the technologies addressed by EN 301 549 and compare its relationship to WCAG.

### I.A.13
**Cognitive Level:** Identify
Identify EN 301 549 requirements beyond WCAG, including Annex A and Web Accessibility Directive implications.

### I.A.14
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Interpret the self-scoping applicability language used in EN 301 549.

### I.A.2
**Cognitive Level:** Explain
Explain the structure of WCAG 2.2, including the POUR principles, guidelines, success criteria, and conformance levels A, AA, and AAA.

### I.A.3
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Differentiate between normative and non-normative materials in WCAG and explain why that distinction matters for conformance.

### I.A.4
**Cognitive Level:** Apply
Apply W3C techniques, including sufficient techniques, failures, and advisory techniques, to specific WCAG success criteria in implementation scenarios.

### I.A.5
**Cognitive Level:** Explain
Explain the rationale for using current WCAG versions and summarize the accessibility impact of changes introduced in WCAG 2.1 and 2.2.

### I.A.6
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Differentiate between normative and non-normative materials in WAI-ARIA and explain why that distinction matters for conformance.

### I.A.7
**Cognitive Level:** Explain
Explain the purpose of WAI-ARIA 1.2 and describe its model of roles, states, and properties.

### I.A.8
**Cognitive Level:** Apply
Determine when WAI-ARIA should be used instead of native HTML and apply the five rules for using ARIA correctly.

### I.A.9
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
Differentiate between normative and non-normative materials in ATAG 2.0 and explain why that distinction matters for conformance.

### I.B.1

**Cognitive Level:** Analyze

### I.B.4
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze

### I.B.5
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze

### I.B.S1
**Cognitive Level:** Analyze
